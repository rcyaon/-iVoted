Missouri

Learn about Missouri's candidates running for the U.S. Senate



Primaries

Missouri will hold its Primaries on August 2nd to determine which candidates can continue to the General Election on November 8th. Numerous candidates remain from Democrat, Republican, and Independent parties. Learn more about the candidates on the Missouri state website linked below or on individual candidate pages.

Learn More

November 8

This fall, voters in Missouri will elect 1 new member to the US Senate filling the seat held by Roy Blunt (R) who took office in 2011. In-person voting will take place at the polling stations on November 8th and absentee voting will begin 6 weeks before election day.
