Register to Vote

Learn about the Midterm Elections and the candidates running for the U.S. Senate

Each state has different rules and regulations regarding voting. Every state except North Dakota requires you to register before showing up to vote. Most states allow you to register online, in person, or by mail. To find more information on how to register to vote in your state, click the button below to visit the official US government website on registering to vote.
