Mission & Purpose

helping young Americans navigate elections by providing data on candidates in their states and how their beliefs line up

Mission

In #iVoted, we are passionate about sharing information about local candidates and where they stand on the issues you care about. It is our mission to inform the youth so they have the tools they need to vote in an informed matter and understand the policies of those representing them in government.


Purpose

#iVoted's problem statement states that "Young Americans are uninformed about where their politicians stand on major issues and therefore a less inclined to vote in elections."
  • We bring users information to determine which politicians best fit their respective views in an unbiased manner, therefore encouraging more voting.
  • Additionally, we hope to supply additional data including voter turn-out and how it influences outcomes.
