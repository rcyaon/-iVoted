Learn about Nevada's candidates running for the U.S. Senate

Catherine Cortez Masto (D)

"I’m running for re-election because you deserve a Senator who will cut through the gridlock and dysfunction in Washington and deliver real results for your family. I’ll work with anyone — Democrats, Republicans, and Independents — to help Nevada's families succeed."
Economy: Raise minimum wage and create jobs.
Immigration: Fix immigration system and protect immigrants.
Crime: Tough on crime to protect local communities.
Abortion: Pro-choice.

Candidate Website

Adam Laxalt (R)

"From defending our country overseas to serving as Nevada's top law enforcement official as Attorney General, I've committed my life to fighting for what's right. That's what Americans do. That's the legacy we have a duty to protect. And that's why I'm running for the United States Senate."
Economy: Lower inflation and spending.
Immigration: Closed border "remain in Mexico" policy.
Crime: Tough on crime to protect local communities.
Abortion: Pro-life.

Candidate Website

This fall, voters in Nevada will elect 1 new member to the US Senate filling the seat held by Catherine Cortez Masto (D) who took office in 2017. In-person voting will take place at the polling stations on November 8th and early voting will take place from October 22nd to November 4th.
