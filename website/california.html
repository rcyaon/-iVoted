Learn about California's candidates running for the U.S. Senate

Alex Padilla (D)

"The proud son of immigrants from Mexico, Senator Alex Padilla believes in giving everyone a fair shot at the American dream. A progressive problem solver, Alex has dedicated his career to finding solutions to the toughest challenges and fighting for communities that are too often left out and left behind."
Environment: Dedicated to clean air, water, and renewable energy.
Healthcare: Working to fix systematic inequalities.
Housing: Working to create affordable housing.
States Rights: Supports federal oversight and right to abortion.

Candidate Website

Mark Meuser (R)

"Mark Meuser is a native Californian with a proven record of fighting for your constitutional rights."
Environment: Encourage private enterprises to innovate solutions.
Healthcare: Does not support COVID-19 protection mandates.
Housing: Supports limited federal financing.
States Rights: Believes issues like abortion should be left to states.
Candidate website does not contain policy issues.

November 8

This fall, voters in California will elect 1 new member to the US Senate filling the seat held by Alex Padilla (D) who took office in 2021 (filling the seat of Kamala Harris). In-person voting will take place at the polling stations on November 8th and early voting will take place from October 10th to November 7th.
