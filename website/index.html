Elections belong to the people

The Importance of Voting

Voting is a vital link between citizens and their democratic representation, providing the mechanism for elections and political change to be made by representing the majority of its population. However, when said citizens aren't voting they are handing political power to those who do not represent them. Through #iVoted, we are giving you the political education you need to be able to vote in an informed manner and do your part to keep American democracy safe, healthy, and functioning.

Midterm Elections

The U.S. Midterm Elections are taking place on November 8, 2022. This November, 35 of 100 Senate seats and all 435 House of Representative seats are on the ballot. Additionally, 36 states will elect Governors. Midterm elections are an incredibly important and powerful time for citizens to cast their votes and influence the nation's democratic processes.
